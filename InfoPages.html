The 2022 GR86 is an impressive little car with a 2.4 liter 4 stroke boxer engine weighing just under three thousand pounds with 228 HP with a 0-60 time of 5.4 seconds and a 1/4 mile of 14 seconds. All this for $27,700 but are rare and can sell well over sticker price.

The 1968-70 charger came in many many verietys weighing around 3200 pounds with a 7 liter 426 V-8 Hemi and 425 HP. This muscle car can go 0-60 in 5.4 seconds and 1/4 mile in 14 seconds. Prices for these now days are in the $60-80k range but to build would be in the $150-200k.

The 2023 Kawasaki Vulcan has a 649cc, liquid-cooled, 4-stroke, DOHC 8-valve parallel twin engine with 50hp only weighing 500 pounds. This bike has a 0-60 time of 4.21 and no one has officiallly tested the 1/4 mile yet because by the time you get there, your at terminal speeds over 100mph. However Kawasaki states that they are capable of hiting it in 12 seconds.

The 2022 Toyota Supra premium comes with a 382 HP, 3 liter, inline 6 engine giving it a 5.1 second 0-60 time and a quarter mile of 13.4 seconds. This stretched hood weighs 3,400 pounds and the sticker cost is $56,135 but can go for $65k depending on the dealership.
